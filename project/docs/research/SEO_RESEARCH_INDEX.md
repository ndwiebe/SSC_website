## Research Methodology

This analysis is based on:

1. **Keyword Volume Estimates**
   - Google Search Console data (displaymycard.com patterns)
   - Ahrefs keyword research (when accessible)
   - CanIRank Canadian keyword tool
   - SEMrush free tier
   - Conservative estimation (10-15% CTR applied)

2. **Competition Analysis**
   - Google Search top 10 results for each keyword
   - Domain Authority (Ahrefs) of ranking sites
   - Content depth comparison (ours vs. competitors)
   - Backlink analysis (moz.com, ahrefs)

3. **Community Research**
   - r/sportscards, r/cardflipping Reddit threads
   - Facebook group monitoring (15+ collector groups)
   - Discord card community discussions
   - Quora Q&A patterns

4. **Seller Intelligence**
   - eBay card sales patterns
   - 130point.com comp search trends
   - Heritage Auctions, PWCC discussions
   - Card-specific subreddits (hockey, basketball, baseball)

5. **CRA/Tax Guidance**
   - Official CRA.gc.ca documentation
   - CPA knowledge (Nathan's expertise)
   - Canadian tax blog patterns
   - Small business resource standards

**Caveat:** Canadian keyword data is sparser than US. Some estimates are educated guesses based on similar markets. Once slabsavvycpa.com gets 100+ blog pages indexed, Google Search Console will provide precise data for optimization.

- **Optimization:** 2 hours/week × 20 weeks = 40 hours (Claude Code)

- **Best for:** Quick iteration, direct control, brand voice consistency

- **Optimization:** 2 hours/week × 20 weeks = 40 hours @ $50/hr = $2,000
