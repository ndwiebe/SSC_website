## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Tax Content Gets Outdated
**Mitigation:** Annual review (May, when CPAs review changes). Add "Updated [Month Year]" to posts. Link to CRA official pages (timeless).

### Risk 2: Posts Don't Rank
**Mitigation:** Use low-competition keywords first. "GST sports cards Canada" has zero established competitor (easy win). Build momentum, then tackle medium-difficulty keywords.

### Risk 3: Blog Traffic Doesn't Convert
**Mitigation:** Soft CTAs, not pushy ones. Link contextually to products (e.g., Tax Playbook mentioned in tax posts, SST in operations posts). Track which posts convert best, optimize.

### Risk 4: Cannibalizes Your Existing Pages
**Mitigation:** Blog posts link TO existing pages, not vice versa. Example: "/blog/gst-guide" links to "/taxready" and "/spreadsheet." You're funneling traffic, not competing.
