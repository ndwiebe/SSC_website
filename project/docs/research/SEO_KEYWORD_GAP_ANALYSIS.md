## Executive Summary

Slab Savvy operates in a **high-intent, underserved niche** where Canadian sports card dealers are actively searching for:
1. Tax & legal guidance (CRA compliance, GST, income classification)
2. Business operations (inventory tracking, pricing strategies, eBay optimization)
3. Market intelligence (comp search, pricing trends, card valuation)

**Major Gap:** No single resource comprehensively serves Canadian card dealers. Competitors are either US-focused, general tax blogs, or fragmented forums. **Opportunity:** 15-20 blog posts (2-4 months of consistent publishing) can capture 40-60% of monthly dealer searches in Canada.

## Part 7: SEO Infrastructure Checklist

Before publishing blog posts, ensure:

### Technical SEO
- [ ] Blog URL structure: `/blog/[slug]` or `/resources/[slug]` (consistent)
- [ ] XML sitemap includes all blog URLs
- [ ] robots.txt allows blog crawling
- [ ] Canonical tags on all blog posts
- [ ] Page speed > 85 LCP (Lighthouse)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly layout (test at 375px viewport)

### On-Page Optimization (Per Post)
- [ ] Title tag: 50-60 chars, primary keyword first
- [ ] Meta description: 150-160 chars, include keyword, CTA
- [ ] H1: Single, primary keyword, compelling
- [ ] H2-H4: Hierarchical, secondary keywords, reading level
- [ ] Image alt text: Descriptive, keyword-natural (not stuffed)
- [ ] Internal links: 3-5 links per 1000 words, context-driven
- [ ] External links: 2-3 authority sources per post
- [ ] Keyword density: Primary keyword 0.5-1.5% (natural)

### Content Markup
- [ ] JSON-LD Article schema (title, author, datePublished, dateModified, image, wordCount)
- [ ] Author credentials (byline: "By Nathan Wiebe, CPA" + bio link)
- [ ] Table of Contents (for long posts 2000+ words)
- [ ] FAQ schema (if applicable)

### Post-Publish
- [ ] Add to sitemap.xml
- [ ] Submit to Google Search Console (manual or via sitemap)
- [ ] Create internal link from relevant existing pages
- [ ] Add to blog index/category page

## Part 12: Risk Mitigation & Content Standards

### Accuracy & Compliance
**Tax content must be reviewed.** Canada's tax system evolves (GST thresholds, small business rules). Recommend:
- [ ] Have accountant/tax lawyer review all "CRA" and "GST" posts before publishing
- [ ] Add disclaimer: "This is educational content. Consult a CPA for your specific situation."
- [ ] Include reference links to official CRA documents
- [ ] Update annually (May post with note about CY updates)

### Avoiding Duplicate Content
- **Unique angle:** Every post should have original insight or Canadian focus
- **Not just eBay tips rehashed:** Position as "Card Dealer Business Guide," not card collecting
- **Verify competitor content:** Before publishing, check if exact keyword already dominates (Ahrefs)

### Attribution & Sources
- Cite sources for data (eBay fee schedules, CRA guidance, 130point.com)
- Link to original research where possible
- Disclose any affiliate relationships
